Planaram

Planaram is a tool designed for beginner League of Legends players to help them use the match loading time to strategize and improve their odds of winning.

To use this tool, simply click the "New Matchup" button to get a new matchup, look at both team compositions, and ask yourself several key questions:

  • What is blue's win condition? How will blue want to play to emphasize that win condition? Do they want to force fights or poke?
  • Similarly, what is red's win condition and how will red want to play, etc.?
  • Given that both teams know each other's win conditions and play styles, which team is likely to initiate the fight? Which champion on that team is likely to initiate, and using what abilities?
  • Imagine you are one of these champions. What role does your champion need to fulfill during the team fights? Is your team going to be waiting for you to initiate? Should you just hang back and poke? Do you need to peel for a specific allied champion?
  • How does the enemy team's scale late game compare to how your team scales late game? Is it in your best interest to force lots of fights and try to end the match quickly, or should you try to draw out the game as long as possible?

By thinking about these questions during the loading screen, you can change your play style from being reactive ("Oh crap, the enemy just went in on us, what should I do?") to proactive ("We knew the Amumu was going to try to bandage toss in and ult; now that he's spent his ult, I know I can focus their Miss Fortune.")

Once you've spent some time thinking up your own answers you can click on the "View community notes" button to see what other people's thought processes were to this question. This opens up a wiki page where the whole community can contribute their ideas.
